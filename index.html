About Us

Best Time to Visit is a single page app made by the team of Uttarakhand Traveller in contribution to Uttarakhand Tourism. It's sole purpose is to provide Best-Time-to-Visit as its the most frequently asked question when someone plans for a visit.

How to Use

Simply start typing the place/destination name and it appears in the dropdown menu and click on it to select it then submit it. If the place exists in our database, it will be shown right away, else a message will tell you its not.

Open for Contribution

When you get detail of any place, it has an option to edit the information. As soon as you click that you have option to submit your knowledge. Once you submit the information, it is sent for approval and once approved it edits the current info for the place. In this way, you can correct us, help Uttarakhand Tourism.
